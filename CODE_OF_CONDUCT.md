# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and reviews.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions, and focus on solutions.
- Accept feedback gracefully and provide feedback with technical clarity.
- Prioritize community safety and inclusion.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, insulting comments, and sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, community chat, and in-person/online events related to the project.

## Reporting

Report incidents to the maintainers by opening a private security/administration contact channel for this repository.

If your project has a dedicated address, replace this line with it (for example: `conduct@your-domain`).

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning, temporary suspension, or permanent ban from project spaces.
